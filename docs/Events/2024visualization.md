# Data Visualization Workshop [Imperial College Business School, 09/05/2024]

**Date:** Thursday, May 9, 2024

**Time:** 12 PM - 2 PM

**Location:** Imperial College Business School [map](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Imperial+College+Business+School/@51.49933,-0.174786,15z/data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x0:0x8bd83d1c5c818a32?sa=X&ved=1t:2428&ictx=111)

On May 9, Imperial College Business School will be hosting a Data Visualization Workshop led by Elizabeth Long Lingo and Ozumcan Demir-Caliskan. This interactive workshop will explore how scholars might utilize visualization to analyse and present complex, multi-level qualitative data and theory in a compelling and parsimonious way. 

If you are interested in participating in this event, please make sure to register [**here**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdgUFgRjWxEENPeJdb90XZcCJOhANpL5K2_nuspZtu2NCTIwQ/viewform) by May 4, 2024.
