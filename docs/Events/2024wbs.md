# Early Career Qualitative Festival [Warwick Business School, 07/05/2024]

**Date:** Tuesday, May 7, 2024

**Time:** 9:30 AM - 1:30 PM

**Location:** Warwick Business School [map](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Warwick+Business+School/@52.3823554,-1.5656233,15z/data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x0:0x6d3da18bcf89f003?sa=X&ved=1t:2428&ictx=111)

On May 7, Warwick Business School will be hosting an Early Career Qualitative Festival. This festival aims at showcasing the qualitative research that is being conducted within our network of institutions. It will include an interactive poster display, a three-minute dissertation competition, and a best conference paper competition. 

If you are interested in participating in this event, please make sure to register [**here**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdq6MY-dTR9go0qFb1azUihy2PmUthFrDt8bM4qq_0yp7SMYg/viewform?pli=1) by April 1, 2024.
